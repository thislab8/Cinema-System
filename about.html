Movie Ticket

A movie ticket booking system is a software application that allows customers to purchase tickets online for movies at local theaters. It is usually integrated with the theater's existing ticketing system and provides customers with an easy-to-use interface for selecting and purchasing tickets. The system typically allows customers to select their movie, theater, showtime, and the number of tickets they wish to purchase. Once the order is placed, a confirmation is sent to the customer. The system also allows theaters to manage their ticket inventory and keep track of customer data.

  • A secure online payment gateway that allows customers to purchase tickets online with their credit or debit cards.
  • A ticket search feature that allows customers to search for available tickets by date, time, and location.
  • Customers should be able to select their own seats for the movie theater.
